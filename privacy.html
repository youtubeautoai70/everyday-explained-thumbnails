Privacy Policy

Everyday Explained ("we", "us") publishes short educational videos to YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.

What this app does

This TikTok app uses TikTok's Content Posting API to publish our own pre-produced videos to our own TikTok account (@everydayexplainedhq). It does not collect, store, or process any data from TikTok users other than the OAuth access token needed to publish videos to our own account.

Data we handle

We store a TikTok OAuth access/refresh token to authenticate publishing requests. We do not collect personal data from viewers or other TikTok accounts.

Contact

For questions, contact us via the TikTok account @everydayexplainedhq.
